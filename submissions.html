General Submissions Open
January 15, 2026 - March 15, 2026
Fiction, Poetry, Nonfiction, and Visual Art Are All Accepted
ANY USE OF AI IS PROHIBITED.
If the editor(s) suspect any use of AI, submission will be refused and the submittor will be banned from submitting to magazine.

FICTION: Please keep your submission between 750-2000 words. No simultaenous submissions. 12-pt, Times New Roman, and double-spaced for legibility must be ascertained or your submission may not be considered.

POETRY: Please submit one to three pieces of poetry, consisting of no more than five pages. No simultaenous submissions. 12-pt, Times New Roman, and double-spaced for legibility must be ascertained or your submission may not be considered.

NONFICTION: Please keep your submission between 750-2000 words. If you have cited sources, please include your cited sources under APA format in a following page after your piece within the same document. The cited sources page(s) will not be included under the word count. No simultaenous submissions. 12-pt, Times New Roman, and double-spaced for legibility must be ascertained or your submission may not be considered.

VISUAL ART: Please submit one to five pieces of visual art for demonstration and inclusion into the magazine. Visual art consists of but is not limited to handdrawn art, photography, collage, graphic design, stills, etc... If chosen for publishing, be aware that not all of the submitted material may be integrated into the demonstration for the sake of space and relevance to adjacent media.

For poetry and prose, a combined max submission cap of 50 will be made. For visual media, a max submission cap of 25 will be made. Due to the limitations of the editor, only so many can be read, so keeping the submissions to these barriers will reduce the stress of review and publishing. Thank you for your understanding, and we look forward to absorbing your art!
